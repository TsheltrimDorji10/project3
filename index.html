About Us

Our mission and values

Who Are We?

We are a vegan non-profit animal rescue organization and sanctuary in Ontario, Canada. We rescue and rehabilitate farm animals from slaughterhouses, laboraties, fur farms, and the entertainment industry by providing lifelong care, where they are able to live the remainder of their lives with respect, dignity, and freedom. We believe that every sentient being has a right to live a life free of suffering, oppression, and human exploitation.

We envision a peaceful world in which all sentient beings are treated with respect, kindness, and respect. We believe that animal rights and other social justice movements are intersectional, and hold regular workshops and events to educate the public on the plight of animals used in different industries.

What We Do

Advocating for the voiceless

Rescue

We rescue abused, abandoned, and/or slaughter-bound animals from slaughterhouses, laboratories, fur farms, and the entertainment industry (e.g. circuses and theme parks). Our medical and rehabilitation staff work closely together to ensure that the animals are nursed back to health, and live a life free of suffering, oppression, and human exploitation.

Education

In alliance with other social justice groups, we hold regular workshops and events to educate others on the plight of animals used in agriculture and other industries. By connecting people with animals and sharing their stories, we advocate for animal rights, reduce suffering, and showcase the intersectionality of animal rights with other social justice movements.
